This project uses images generated from data on city locations and their weather. Using a uniform probability distribution, cities were obtained using randomised lat and lon coordinates. For each city: its maxiumum temperature, the level of cloudiness, wind speed and humidity were obtained. The following images show the relation between the distribution of cities and their weather, and following that the effect of each locations proximity to the equator.
